Topic 2

Site Topic #2: Auroras: An aurora, also known as the polar lights are natural lights appearing in the earths sky. They mainly appear in high-latitude regions. The lights show different patterns of colorful lights that resemble curtains, spirals, rays of light, and different flickering lights taking up the sky.

Site Purpose: To educate and share information about anyone that is curious about aurora lights.
